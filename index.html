Our philosophy

We don't replace people. We remove everything that prevents them from doing their best work.

Every trade we've studied has the same shape: real expertise, buried under decades of paperwork, re-keying, and workarounds nobody chose on purpose. That's not craft. That's friction wearing craft's clothes.

Recrafted isn't automation software, and it isn't a robot standing in for a person. It's the un-friction — built by watching how underwriters, inspectors, machinists and cooks actually work, then teaching AI to do the tedious part.

How it works

01

Study the workflow

We sit with the people doing the work — reading files, walking floors, watching the real steps, not the org chart's version of them.

02

Automate the tedium

AI takes on the re-keying, the cross-referencing, the paperwork — the parts that were never the craft to begin with.

03

Return the craft

The expert makes the judgment call again — underwriting the risk, approving the permit, plating the dish — with hours back to do it well.
